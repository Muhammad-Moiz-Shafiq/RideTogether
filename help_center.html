Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?
Booking Steps
  1. Enter your route details and search
  2. Browse available rides matching your criteria
  3. Select a ride and send a booking request
  4. Wait for driver confirmation (usually within 24 hours)
  5. Once confirmed, you'll get contact details and meeting point
Learn More
Cancellation Policy
Cancellation Policy

Free cancellations up to 3 hours before departure. Last-minute cancellations may result in a small fee to compensate the driver for lost opportunity.

Learn More
How much does it cost?
Cost Information

Prices are set by drivers but average 50-75% less than taxis. Most rides within Islamabad/Rawalpindi range from Rs. 200-400 depending on distance.

Learn More
Is RideTogether safe?
Safety Features

Absolutely! All users must verify their NUST student ID. We also have a rating system and emergency support available 24/7 during active rides.

Learn More
What about insurance?
Insurance Coverage

Drivers must have valid vehicle insurance. For added peace of mind, RideTogether provides complementary ride protection for all verified trips.

Learn More
Is there a mobile app?
Mobile App Availability

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. Download it for real-time ride tracking, instant notifications, and quick booking.
